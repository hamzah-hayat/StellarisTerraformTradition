# Product: Stellaris Terraform Tradition

A Stellaris mod that adds a complete tradition tree focused on terraforming, planetary management, and Gaia world creation.

## Key Features
- Custom tradition tree ("Terraform") with adopt/finish effects and 5 tradition perks
- Planetary decisions for adding basic and strategic resource deposits
- "Creator of Worlds" ascension perk for marking uninhabitable planets as terraforming candidates and repairing shattered worlds
- "New Worlds" council agenda that creates habitable planets in sectors
- Overrides vanilla World Shaper and Detox ascension perks to prevent stacking with this tradition
- Custom events, modifiers, terraform links, and species traits

## Target Game Version
Stellaris v4.3.* (Cetus patch)

## Steam Workshop
Published as "Terraform Tradition" (Workshop ID: 3683266184)

## Design Philosophy
- Rewards empires that invest in terraforming tech and plan to create Gaia worlds
- Balanced around mid-to-late game progression (requires tech prerequisites)
- AI-aware: includes ai_weight values for decisions and perks
- Avoids conflicts with vanilla by overriding only necessary files (ascension perks, system tooltips, game rules)
